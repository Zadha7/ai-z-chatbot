Frequently Asked Questions

Before contacting us, you might find the answer to your question below:

Is Free ITMT Tool really completely free?

Yes! All our tools are 100% free to use with no hidden fees, subscription plans, or premium features. You can use any tool unlimited times without paying anything.

Do I need to create an account to use the tools?

No account is required. Simply visit any tool page and start using it immediately. We don't require registration, sign-ups, or email addresses.

Are my files safe? Do you store them on your servers?

Your files are completely safe. All processing happens directly in your browser using client-side JavaScript. Your images, PDFs, and text never leave your device, and we don't store or access any of your files.

What file size limits do you have?

For optimal performance, we recommend: images under 10MB, PDFs under 50MB. These limits ensure fast processing while working within browser memory constraints.

Which browsers are supported?

Free ITMT Tool works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera. We recommend using the latest version of your preferred browser for the best experience.

Can I use these tools on my mobile device?

Absolutely! All our tools are fully responsive and work great on smartphones and tablets. Simply visit our website from your mobile browser.

A tool isn't working properly. What should I do?

First, try refreshing the page and clearing your browser cache. If the problem persists, please contact us with details about the issue, including your browser type, operating system, and steps to reproduce the problem.

Can you add a specific feature or new tool?

We love feature suggestions! Please use the contact form above to submit your ideas. While we can't implement every request, we carefully consider all feedback and prioritize features that benefit the most users.

How do I report a bug or security issue?

Please email us immediately at support@freeitmttool.com with "BUG REPORT" or "SECURITY" in the subject line. Include detailed information about the issue so we can address it quickly.

Do you offer business or commercial licenses?

Free ITMT Tool is free for personal, educational, and commercial use. You don't need a special license to use our tools for business purposes.
