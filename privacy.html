Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Our Privacy Commitment

At Free ITMT Tool, your privacy is our highest priority. All file processing happens directly in your browser. Your images, PDFs, and documents never leave your device and are never uploaded to our servers. We don't store, view, or access any of your files.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Free ITMT Tool ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you visit our website and use our services (collectively, the "Service"). By using Free ITMT Tool, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Free ITMT Tool is designed with privacy-first architecture. We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

  • Your Files: All images, PDFs, and documents you process remain entirely on your device. We never upload, store, or transmit your files to our servers.
  • File Contents: We never access, view, analyze, or store the content of your files.
  • Personal Identification: We do not require registration, so we don't collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personally identifiable information.
  • User Accounts: We don't maintain user accounts or profiles.
  • Payment Information: Our service is completely free, so we never collect credit card or payment information.

3. Information We May Collect

While we don't collect your files or personal data, we may collect limited, non-personal information to maintain and improve our service:

3.1 Automatically Collected Information

  • Technical Data: Browser type and version, device type, operating system, screen resolution, and language preferences
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, clickstream data, and general interaction patterns
  • Performance Data: Error messages, loading times, and service reliability metrics

3.2 Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality and security
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our service (anonymous, aggregated data only)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences for future visits

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may limit some functionality.

4. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • Optimize website performance and user experience
  • Understand which tools are most popular and valuable
  • Ensure service security and prevent abuse

5. Client-Side Processing

All file processing operations in Free ITMT Tool use client-side JavaScript, which means:

  • Processing happens entirely within your web browser
  • Files are processed using your device's memory and processing power
  • No files are uploaded to, transmitted to, or stored on any server
  • When you download processed files, they come directly from your browser's memory
  • Closing your browser or navigating away permanently removes all temporary data

This architecture ensures maximum privacy and security for your sensitive files.

6. Third-Party Services

Free ITMT Tool uses the following types of third-party services:

6.1 JavaScript Libraries

We use open-source libraries delivered via CDN (Content Delivery Network) for tool functionality:

  • Tesseract.js: OCR (Optical Character Recognition) for image-to-text conversion
  • jsPDF: PDF generation and manipulation
  • PDF-Lib: PDF merging and processing
  • Font Awesome: Icons and visual elements

These libraries process data locally in your browser and do not transmit your files elsewhere.

6.2 Analytics Services (If Applicable)

We may use privacy-focused analytics services (such as Google Analytics with IP anonymization) to understand website traffic and usage patterns. These services:

  • Only collect anonymous, aggregated data
  • Do not track individual users
  • Do not access or monitor your files
  • Comply with data protection regulations

6.3 Hosting and Infrastructure

Our website is hosted on secure, reliable infrastructure providers. These providers:

  • Only deliver the website code (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) to your browser
  • Do not receive, process, or store any files you work with
  • Maintain industry-standard security practices

7. Data Retention

Since we don't collect or store your files or personal information, there is no data to retain. Any technical or usage data we collect is:

  • Anonymous and aggregated
  • Retained only as long as necessary for service improvement
  • Automatically deleted after a reasonable period
  • Never linked to individual users

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect the Service:

  • HTTPS Encryption: Secure connection between your browser and our website
  • Client-Side Processing: Your files never leave your device
  • No Data Storage: No file databases or storage systems that could be compromised
  • Regular Updates: We keep all libraries and dependencies up to date with security patches
  • Secure Development: Following best practices for web security

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Because we don't collect personal data or store your files, most traditional privacy rights don't apply. However, you have the right to:

  • Use the Service Anonymously: No registration or identification required
  • Control Cookies: Manage cookie preferences through your browser
  • Request Information: Contact us to learn about our privacy practices
  • Clear Browser Data: Delete any locally stored preferences or cache
  • Stop Using the Service: Discontinue use at any time without consequences

10. Children's Privacy

Free ITMT Tool does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. Since we don't require registration or collect personal information, children can safely use our tools under parental supervision. Parents and guardians should monitor their children's internet activities.

11. International Users

Free ITMT Tool is accessible worldwide. By using our service, you acknowledge that:

  • All processing occurs locally in your browser regardless of your location
  • Any minimal data we collect may be processed in various jurisdictions
  • We comply with applicable data protection regulations including GDPR and CCPA principles
  • Your files never cross international borders as they never leave your device

12. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers include "Do Not Track" (DNT) features. While we respect user privacy preferences, the limited data we collect (anonymous analytics) is essential for service operation and improvement. We encourage users to use browser privacy features and extensions for additional control.

13. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Highlighting major changes in a notice on our homepage

Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

14. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional privacy rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). However, because Free ITMT Tool:

  • Does not collect or sell personal information
  • Does not store user files or data
  • Only collects minimal, anonymous usage statistics

Most CCPA provisions do not apply to our service. California residents may still contact us with privacy questions.

15. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

We respect the privacy rights of European Union users under GDPR. Our privacy-first architecture ensures compliance:

  • Data Minimization: We collect only essential, non-personal data
  • Purpose Limitation: Data used only for stated purposes
  • No Personal Data Processing: Your files are processed locally, not on our servers
  • Right to Access: Contact us for information about data practices
  • Data Portability: Not applicable as we don't store personal data

16. External Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party websites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

17. Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, any limited data we hold may be transferred to the acquiring entity. This policy will continue to apply, and we will notify users of any ownership changes.

18. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@freeitmttool.com
  • Contact Page: Visit our Contact Page

We will respond to privacy inquiries within a reasonable timeframe.

Summary: Free ITMT Tool is built on privacy-first principles. Your files are processed entirely in your browser and never uploaded to our servers. We don't collect personal information, maintain user accounts, or store your data. Use our tools with confidence knowing your privacy is protected.
